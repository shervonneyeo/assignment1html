Introduction and description of Singapore's Zoo
Read on for interesting facts!

Singapore's zoo is wellknown worldwide for it's "Open Concept" .
Singapore's zoo also allows you to see for yourself in action more than
2400 specimens of over 300 different species!

Did you know?
34% of these species were threatened.
Singapore's zoo thus maintains its strong reputation internationally for its conservation initiatives and breeding programmes.

Also, in order to continuously meet the healthcare needs of its animals and working towards its apriration
of becoming the global centre of excellence for veterinary healthcare and research,
a purpose-built Wildlife Healthcare and Research Centre was even set up in March 2006!

What are you waiting for?
Be part of the 1.9 million visitors annually
who enjoy their day of experiential learning journey at the 26-hectare award-winning Zoo.

  • Show timings

    • Catch our special shows at the Zoo to witness the amazing talents of our animals and our trainers!
    Frequency of shows: 2 x each daily
    Duration of shows: 15 - 20 mins each
    Do note that shows availability are subjected to good weather conditions.

    Show Location Morning Show Afternoon Show
    Splash Safari Shaw Foundation Amphitheatre 10.30am 5.00pm
    Animal Friends Rainforest Kidzworld Amphitheatre 1.30pm 4.00pm
    Rainforest Fights back Shaw Foundation Amphitheatre 12.00pm 2.30pm

    Some tips and tricks to get the most experience out of the shows!
    1. Arrive early to avoid disappointment
    As seats capacity are limited, do arrive earlier to avoid trouble finding a seat for the show.

    2. Get ready for a splashing good time!
    Occupy seats marked with splash zones area only if you do not mind getting splashed.

    3. Flash off, eyes on!
    Please refrain from flash photography as it may disrupt the animals during the shows.

    4. Stay behind!
    There are usually photography sessions scheduled at the end of the show, so stay on if you want to have your very own momento with the animals!
